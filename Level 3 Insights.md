# **Level 3 – Advanced \& Strategic Insights**



1\. Top Customers by Spend



Insight:

The top 5 customers contribute ₹41,452, accounting for approximately 0.3% of total revenue. This indicates that revenue is not overly concentrated among a few customers, reducing dependency risk but also highlighting an opportunity to further nurture high-value customers.



2\. Month-over-Month Revenue Growth



Insight:

Revenue shows high volatility, growing by 105% in February 2017 but declining by 54% in March 2017. This fluctuation suggests demand sensitivity to seasonal or promotional factors, emphasizing the importance of consistent customer engagement strategies.



3\. Repeat Customer Revenue Contribution



Insight:

Repeat customers generate ₹728,409, contributing 5.35% of total revenue. Although repeat customers form a smaller segment, their disproportionate revenue contribution highlights the strong business impact of customer retention initiatives.



4\. Category Ranking by Month



Insight:

The category “moveis\_decoracao” consistently ranks among the top 3 categories across multiple months, indicating stable and sustained demand compared to more seasonal product categories.



5\. Running Total Revenue



Insight:

Cumulative revenue crosses ₹13,221,498 by August 2018, showing that a significant portion of total revenue is generated steadily over time rather than in isolated spikes, reflecting long-term business sustainability.



6\. Top Product per Category



Insight:

Different products lead revenue within each category, indicating that sales are not dependent on a single product. This diversification reduces product-level risk and suggests healthy competition within categories.



7\. Customers Above Average Order Value



Insight:

A total of 26,711 customers spend above the average order value, making them prime candidates for loyalty programs, personalized offers, and premium targeting to further increase lifetime value.
