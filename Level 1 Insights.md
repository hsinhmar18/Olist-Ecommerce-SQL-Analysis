# **Level 1 – Basic Business Insights**

1\. Total Customers



Insight:

The platform has 96,096 unique customers, indicating a strong customer base. However, only a small proportion of customers place repeat orders, suggesting a clear opportunity to improve customer retention strategies.



2\. Total Orders



Insight:

A total of 99,441 orders have been placed, reflecting steady transactional activity and consistent customer demand on the platform.



3\. Order Status Distribution



Insight:

Out of 99,441 total orders:



96,478 orders (≈97.3%) are delivered



625 orders (≈0.6%) are cancelled



This indicates a high fulfillment success rate, though even a small cancellation percentage can lead to avoidable revenue and operational losses.



4\. Top Cities by Orders



Insight:

The top 3 cities by order volume are:



São Paulo



Rio de Janeiro



Belo Horizonte



These cities contribute a significant share of total orders, indicating high urban demand concentration. Prioritizing logistics and targeted marketing in these cities could further improve performance.



5\. Total Revenue



Insight:

The platform generated a total revenue of 13,591,643.70, driven primarily by a high volume of individual item sales rather than bulk purchases. This highlights the importance of order frequency over order size.



6\. Average Order Value (AOV)



Insight:

The average order value is 137.75, indicating moderate per-order spending. Even a small increase in AOV through bundling, cross-selling, or recommendations could significantly increase overall revenue.



7\. Top Product Categories by Sales Volume



Insight:

The top 5 product categories by items sold are:



cama\_mesa\_banho



beleza\_saude



esporte\_lazer



moveis\_decoracao



informatica\_acessorios



These categories dominate sales volume, reflecting strong customer preference and making them ideal candidates for promotions and inventory optimization.



8\. Payment Methods



Insight:

The top 3 most used payment methods are:



credit\_card



boleto



voucher



This shows a strong preference for digital and flexible payment options, emphasizing the need for a seamless and reliable payment experience.
